Lasagna

Description

This magnificent Italian dish, the lasagna, is a fairly easy-to-make delicious recipe. It consists of cooking a few ingredients such as the meat and noodles, and properly layering the lasagna with cheese, meat, and the noodles.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb sweet italian sausage
  • 3/4 lb lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 crushed garlic cloves
  • 1 (28oz) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6.5oz) cans canned tomato sauce
  • 2 (6oz) cans of tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tbsp white sugar
  • 1 tsp italian seasoning
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp fennel seeds
  • 1/4 tsp ground black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 lb mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
