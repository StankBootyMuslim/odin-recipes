Pizza

Description

Pizza, simple but delicious. Consisting of dough, pizza sauce, cheese, any toppings you desire, and off to the oven. In this recipe, the Margherita Pizza will be showcased!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 heaping cup pizza sauce
  • 8 oz fresh bocconcini mozzarella, sliced
  • 1/2 cup sliced cherry tomatoes
  • 10 basil leaves
  • Pinch of red pepper flakes
  • Drizzle of extra-virgin olive-oil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 500 degrees Farenheight.
  2. Spread pizza sauce onto the dough.
  3. Top with fresh mozzarella and tomatoes.
  4. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until crust is golden brown.
  5. Remove from oven and top with fresh basil leaves, a pinch of red pepper flakes and a drizzle of olive oil.
